MathsTutorHelp is committed to providing a safe, respectful and supportive learning environment for every student, whether tuition takes place online, in person or in a group class.

## Our Commitment

- Mr Sheraz holds an enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check, confirming he is suitable to work with children
- In-person group classes take place in a supervised setting at Levenshulme High School or another confirmed tuition venue, never in a private, unsupervised location
- Online one-to-one sessions are conducted through an agreed online platform, and we encourage a parent or guardian to be aware of when sessions take place
- We do not communicate with students privately outside lesson time or agreed contact channels; WhatsApp messages relating to lessons are normally sent to the parent or guardian's number, not directly to a child under 16 unless otherwise agreed

## If You Have a Concern

If you have any safeguarding concern about a lesson, a member of staff or anything else related to a student's welfare, please contact us directly and as soon as possible:

- Phone/WhatsApp: 07860 248525
- Contact page: [mathstutorhelp.com/contact/](/contact/)

We take all concerns seriously and will respond promptly.

## Reporting Outside MathsTutorHelp

If you believe a child is at immediate risk of harm, please contact:

- **Police:** 999 in an emergency or 101 for a non-emergency
- **NSPCC Helpline:** 0808 800 5000
- **Local authority children's services:** contact the relevant local authority for the child's area

## Our Approach

- We follow the principle that safeguarding is everyone's responsibility
- Any concern raised about a student's wellbeing, even if unrelated to tuition, will be taken seriously and, where appropriate, escalated to the relevant local authority or agency
- We keep any safeguarding-related records securely and confidentially, in line with our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/)
